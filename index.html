John and Duday's Romantic Honeymoon in Kuala Lumpur

Day 1: Arrival, Batu Caves, and Bukit Bintang

Arrive at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA).

Take the KLIA Express to KL Sentral Station.

Day 2: Petronas Twin Towers, Perdana Botanical Gardens, and Chinatown

Visit the iconic Petronas Twin Towers, the tallest twin towers in the world.

Day 3: Genting Highlands and Resorts World Genting Theme Park

Take a bus or cable car to Genting Highlands.

Day 4: Departure

Check out of your hotel and take the KLIA Express to the airport.

Catch your flight back to Phnom Penh.

Budget

  • Accommodation: Face Suites (approximately $25 per night)
  • Transportation: KLIA Express ($15), KTM Komuter ($0.25), buses (variable), Grab or Uber (variable)
  • Food: Street food (approximately $3-5 per meal)
